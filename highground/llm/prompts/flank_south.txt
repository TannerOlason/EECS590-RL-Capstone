Execute a southern flanking manoeuvre. Route all three units through the southern edge of the map to approach the enemy from below their current position. Prioritise reaching tiles that are at least 2 rows south of the enemy centroid before committing to a full engagement. Use moderate attack urgency only when a unit is already adjacent to an enemy during the flank; otherwise keep moving. This phase is complete when your units are established south of the enemy line and ready to attack from that angle.
