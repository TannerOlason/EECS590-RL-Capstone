Split the team's attention between two simultaneous objectives: send one or two units to occupy high ground while the remaining unit engages the nearest enemy directly. The front-line unit should use high attack urgency to pin the enemy in place while the flanking units secure elevated terrain. Once the high ground is taken, converge all units toward the pinned enemy for a coordinated finishing blow. This phase is complete when you hold at least one elevation-2 tile and have eliminated or severely damaged the nearest enemy.
